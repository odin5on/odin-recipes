Cereal

Description

This delicacy is one of my personal favorites for breakfast. It consists of a bite-sized wheat biscuit coated on top with white sugar.

Ingredients

  • Frosted Mini Wheats Cereal
  • Milk (Whole milk preffered)
  • Bowl
  • Spoon

Steps

  1. Get out bowl and spoon. Set spoon aside.
  2. Pour Frosted Mini Wheats Cereal in bowl, about three-quarters full, depending on size of bowl
  3. Pour milk over the cereal unitl cereal starts to float
  4. Eat cereal with spoon. Enjoy!
