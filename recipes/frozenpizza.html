Frozen Pizza

Description

This delicacy is one of my personal favorites for dinner. It consists of a 12 inch diameter crust topped with cheese and pepperoni.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Pizza
  • Baking Sheet
  • Pizza Cutter
  • Paper Plate

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Once oven is at temperature, place pizza on baking sheet and place in the oven
  3. Set timer for 12-15 min
  4. Take pizza out of oven once the cheese on top starts to turn golden brown
  5. Cut pizza into slices using pizza cutter
  6. Eat from paper plate. Enjoy!
