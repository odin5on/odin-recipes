Chicken

Description

This delicacy is one of my personal favorites for dinner. It consists of a chicken breast cooked on the stove with olive oil, salt and pepper.

Ingredients

  • Raw Chicken Breast
  • Frying Pan
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat up frying pan with some olive oil
  2. Pound chicken breast with fists to soften
  3. Spread olive oil over the entire chicken breast
  4. Sprinkle salt and pepper on both sides of chicken breast
  5. Put chicken breast on frying pan
  6. Flip chicken once the edges start to turn white
  7. Remove chicken once it looks cooked and internal temp has reached 165 degrees
  8. Eat chicken. Enjoy!
